## Transferable rules (→ LEARNINGS)
1. **Directional COHERENCE wins in the located-figures genre.** Give the figures a shared diagonal
   current (unified shear/lean, #15) or bold crossing diagonals (#4). **Scattered orientation loses**
   (#2/#10 excluded) — this is flow>scatter (R11) applied to figure angle. Keep orientation spread
   LOW when shearing.
2. **Dense woven is a hit** (#14) — density confirmed again, now that "light" is dropped.
3. **The balanced/middle option loses** (#16 "balanced showcase" excluded) — every design should
   commit to one strong idea; don't hedge to the safe middle.
4. For #274 (six geometric figures): mix figure types but keep a **coherent composition / directional
   current**, dense webs, no scattered-tame hedge.
