## ⚠️ The recurring signal — GENRE fatigue
"So many are similar" — said in some form in R3, R6, and now R7. The deeper cause: **rounds 1–7
are all the same visual family** (fill regions with ruled/hatched lines). Jitter, tonal gradients,
and scatter add variety *within* that family, but the family itself is now well-explored and reads
as "more of the same." The fix is not more within-genre variety — it's **genuinely different
genres**. #51 ("architectural points connected by straight lines") is a network/web genre — lean
into it.

   prioritise instructions that change the visual language (webs, arcs, figures, scribbles).
